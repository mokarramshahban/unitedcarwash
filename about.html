Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average cost of a basic car service in India?

The average cost for a basic car service in India ranges from ₹2,000 to ₹5,000 depending on the car brand, model, and location. It typically includes oil change, filter replacements, and a general inspection.

Are there any additional charges apart from service fees?

Yes, there might be additional charges for spare parts, engine oil, or other consumables not included in the base package. Always confirm with your service provider. Taxes (GST) may also apply.

Does regular servicing improve mileage or performance?

Absolutely. Regular servicing ensures that all engine components function efficiently, leading to better mileage, smooth performance, and reduced wear and tear in the long run.

Can proper servicing increase resale value?

Yes, maintaining a proper service record and ensuring timely servicing can significantly boost your car’s resale value. Buyers often look for cars with consistent service history.
